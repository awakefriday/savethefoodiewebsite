What is the confusion?

Many people are unclear of what a foodie really is.They
usually associate them with other food ethusiasts. Are
they a ravening gourmand? Or perhaps a fastidious food
snob? Maybe a curious epicure?

Why is there confusion?

People who have been labeled in the foodie category
have different interpretations as to what it implies.
Some never cared about the true meaning and just go
on to do anything food related whether it is taking
pictures of food or wrting about it. Hence, it feels like
there is no clear meaning to what really is a 'foodie'.
